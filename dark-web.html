What is the Dark Web?

The Dark Web is a part of the internet that requires specific software, configurations, or authorization to access. It's not inherently illegal, but it operates with strong privacy and anonymity protections that make it attractive to both legitimate users and criminals.

The Three Layers of the Internet

ğŸŒ SURFACE WEB (95% of internet)

ğŸ”’ DEEP WEB (4.9% of internet)

ğŸ•·ï¸ DARK WEB (0.1% of internet)

ğŸ“Œ Key Point

The Tor Network (The Onion Router)

Tor is a free, open-source software that enables anonymous communication by directing internet traffic through a series of volunteer-operated servers called nodes.
